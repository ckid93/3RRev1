Airtel Xtreme 4K Box - A User Overview

Ports

The box has two ports on the left side; SD and USB.On the rear side it has another USB port, an optical audio port, HDMI, Ethernet
and Analogue audio, video ports along with the SAT IN for the DTH cable. On the front side it has a power button with TV and Box power indications.
Check out our image gallery for photos of the box and ports.

Remote Control

The accompanying Bluetooth remote has all regular buttons with dedicated buttons for Netflix, Youtube and Prime Video.
There is a dedicated message button for messages from Airtel. The built quality of the remote is very good and feedback is sweet.
Check out our image gallery for photos of the remote control.

Functionality

The box interface features a home page and separate sections for TV channels, Apps, Store, Movies and Sports.
Pressing the back buttom from the home page will take you to the DTH apart from the TV channels section.
While viewing channels, the left navigation button opens the favourite channels. The right button opens the installed apps too.
Check out our image gallery for photos of the interface.

3R Verdict

Reasons to Buy

1. The box has both DTH and OTT apps which covers a complete entertainment experience.
2. The current DTH pack and channels can be viewed and modified from the Store app.
3. 4K support and Optical port are added advantages.

Points to Improve

1. Some apps open directly from the Xtreme App while some need installation and login separately.
2. A dedicated button for DTH would be helpful instead of going to the home page all the time.
3. A USB-C port could have been added to connect SSDs.
4. There is content syncing issues with the box and the Xtreme App on smartphones.
5. Like in other android boxes the interface is a bit laggy at times.
